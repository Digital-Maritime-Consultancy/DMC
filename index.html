About Us

Digital Maritime Consultancy (DMC) is a small company established in 2017, with the sole purpose of working with the Maritime Connectivity Platform (MCP). We work with both the open source reference implementation of the MCP, and are involved in the development of services (in particular service specifications) for the platform.

Employees of DMC have had key roles in different large project working on MCP, mainly the EU projects EfficienSea2 and STM validation project, and the Korean SMART Navigation project. We are also involved in the MCP consortium and relevant international organisations such as IALA and IHO.
