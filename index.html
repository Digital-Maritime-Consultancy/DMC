Who we are

Digital Maritime Consultancy (DMC) is heading towards an exciting future, focusing on providing operational Maritime Connectivity Platform (MCP) services, offering digital maritime services related to weather and navigation information, and actively contributing to standardization efforts in the maritime digitalization arena.

DMC is all about embracing innovation, leveraging data insights, and fostering collaborations to bring about transformative changes in the maritime industry. Our goal is to create a future where efficiency, safety, and sustainability go hand in hand, pushing the boundaries of success and resilience in this dynamic field.

What we do

DMC is a leading provider of comprehensive MCP solutions and expert consultancy services, catering to clients worldwide.

Our main offering includes:

  • Hosting MCP Services: As a reliable host for MCP services, we consistently deliver robust, secure, and scalable platform solutions for maritime organizations, ensuring seamless operations and efficient communication.
  • Expert Assistance in MCP Integration: To maximize the benefits of the MCP, we guide our clients through the process of integrating the platform into their existing services and systems, from initial setup to ongoing technical support.
  • System Architecture and Digitalization Strategy Consultancy: We offer a team of experienced consultants, specializing in system architecture and digitalization strategies for the maritime industry.
  • Compliance with IALA G1128 Service Specification: We assist clients in developing service specifications that comply with IALA's G1128.

History

DMC was established in 2017 with the main purpose of supporting the realisation of the MCP. DMC has also been very active on the technical level of virtually all aspects of MCP - but in particular with regard to the implementation of the MCP Identity registry, which has been primarily carried out by DMC.

Employees of DMC have had key roles in different large project working on MCP, mainly the EU projects EfficienSea2 and STM validation project, and the Korean SMART Navigation project. We are also involved in the MCP consortium and relevant international organisations such as IALA and IHO.

Apart from MCP, DMC is also involved in the development of standardises services in within the maritime domain. These include VTS services, and services for delivering AtoN information and Navigational Warnings.
